Our History

Founded in 1990, our car museum has grown into a time capsule of automotive excellence. Started by a group of car enthusiastic friends to showcase their love for cars to the rest of the world. This museum unveils the past, from Model T's to cutting-edge supercars, honoring a century of innovation.
